Introduction

The Natural Seven Wonders of the World is a list that groups the seven most remarkable, beautiful and extraodinary natural landscapes around the 5 continents. The purpose of the selection of Seven Natural Wonders is connected with the protection and promotion of them as a world heritage that should be respected and enviromentally preserved. There are subdivisions regarding of the considered Seven Wonders in each continent of the world

  1. Seven Natural Wonders of Africa
  2. Seven Natural Wonders of North America
  3. Seven Natural Wonders of Europe
  4. Seven Natural Wonders of Asia

  6. Seven Natural Wonders of South America
  7. Seven Natural Wonders by country

There has been a large debate respecting the parameters of the selection such as how many wonders should be chosen and also the period between each selection. The present Seven Wonders of the World of the year 2013 are the following:

  1. Aurora Borealis
  2. Grand Canyon
  3. Harbour of Rio de Janeiro
  4. Paricutin
  5. Victoria Falls
  6. Mountain Everest
  7. Great Barrier Reef
