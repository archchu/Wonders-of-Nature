Great Barrier Reef

  • The last of the 7 wonders of the world is the Great Barrier Reef along the northeast coast of Queensland, Australia.

  • The Great Barrier Reef stretches 2,600 kilometres and is the world's largest coral reef ecosystem composed of 2,900individual reefs.

  • It supports a variety of vulnerable and endangered species.

  • The Great Barrier Reef covers an area of approximately 344,400 square kilometres and is the only living organism on earth that is visible from space.
