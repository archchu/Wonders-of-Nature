Victoria Waterfalls

  • In Southern Africa, the Zambezi River flows across a flat plateau that extends hundreds of kilometres in all directions.

  • It is here that one will find the largest waterfall in the world

  • Victoria Falls is one of the wonders of the world, stretching 1.7 kilometres wide and shared by the countries ofZambia and Zimbabwe.

  • The falls are formed as the full width of the Zambezi River plummets into a 108 metre high cleft.
